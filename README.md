## Description
>MealPrep Match is an app that helps customers connect with mealprep providers. Customers can choose any >meal plan out of a number of options, they can also add their own recipes. Providers can add meal plans >and recipes. This app simplifies the process of finding better food options and choosing healthier >eating habits.

## App Functions
1. Customer (the user with the customer role):
    1. Create/modify customer profile - Register as a customer and define  preferences, goals, and assess current eating habits.
    2. View available services - Go through plans that are filtered by your own preferences
    3. Subscribe to available services - Customers can select a provider, choose a meal plan and complete the subscription.
    4. Write reviews for subscribed services - After making a purchase, customers are able to submit a written review.
2. Provider (the user with the provider role):
    1. Create/modify/remove provider profile - Register as a provider. add tags to show what is offered (vegan, carnivore, mealprep, recipe, etc). This allows customers to choose providers based on preferences. Providers will need to be verified before being able to submit subscription based content
    2. Create services - Recipes(steps), Mealkits(ingredients and steps) and MealPrep guides (steps for schedule) can all be published by provider. Manage pricing of content and offer a variety of free and paid options.
    3. View customer statistics -  View active subscribers, see overall ratings for a service, have a views to saves statistic (VTS) for recipes and mealprep guides.
    4. Reply to reviews - Providers can access reviews through their posted services, with the ability to reply to reviews (customers cannot reply to other reviews).
